Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the facial recognition?

Our AI model achieves 99.7% accuracy in normal classroom lighting conditions with anti-spoofing protection to prevent fraud.

Can we use it without internet?

Yes! MirrorMind supports on-premise deployment. All processing happens locally, and data syncs when connection is restored.

What camera hardware do we need?

Any standard 720p or higher webcam works. For larger classrooms, we recommend 1080p cameras with wide-angle lenses.

How long is data retained?

You control the data retention period. Default is 30 days, but you can configure it from 7 days to permanent storage.

Is there a mobile app?

Yes! MirrorMind is fully responsive and works on all devices. Native iOS and Android apps are coming soon.

What about student privacy?

We're GDPR and COPPA compliant. All data is encrypted, and parents can request data deletion anytime.
